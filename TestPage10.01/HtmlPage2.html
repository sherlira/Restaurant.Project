Cook-Shef

Chef Nathan was born in the capital of Georgia, Tbilisi. It was there that the interest in cooking and gastronomy in general arose. He started working in restaurant kitchens at the age of 25. His last place of work in Georgia was the Barbarestan restaurant.Nathan revolutionized Georgian cuisine. Using the knowledge gained in his native country, studying and working with star chefs in Europe, he created new gastronomic masterpieces on the foundation of traditional, authentic cuisine.
